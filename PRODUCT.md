# Product

## Product Purpose
Demo authentication app built as a university frontend development exercise (Pràctica 7). Shows React concepts: AuthContext, protected routes, login flow, and user session management.

## Users
Students and professors reviewing the assignment. Viewed on a laptop in a bright classroom or at a desk during daytime. Needs to be legible, professional, and demonstrate good frontend craft.

## Register
product

## Brand tone
Clean, academic, confident. Not corporate SaaS. Not playful startup. Competent student work that takes design seriously.

## Anti-references
- Generic purple SaaS dashboards (Vercel, Linear clones)
- Glassmorphism and neon dark mode
- Identical 3-card dashboard grids with icon + heading + value
- Heavy rounded-square icon tiles above headings

## Color strategy
Committed — one saturated teal carries the identity. Warm stone neutrals tinted toward the brand hue.
